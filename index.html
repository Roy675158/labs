Christmas Punch

Christmas but with a punch to it - Creator Unknown

    Ingredients
  • 2 Cups Orange Juice
  • 2 Cups of Tequila
  • 1 Cup Cranberry Juice
  • 1 Cup Pineapple Juice
  • 1 Cup Ginger Ale
  • Whipped Cream
      Steps
    • Pour 2 cuprs of orange juice into a large pitcher
    • Pour 2 cups of your favorite Tequila
    • Add 1 cup of Cranberry Juice
    • Add 1 cup of Ginger ale soda
    • Mix ingredients well then taste
    • Add whipped cream on top
